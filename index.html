ABOUT ME

I started my journey in the data area in 2015 using Excel in a family business, where I was responsible for building and maintaining several support files. In 2017 I became a Marketing intern with the aim of controlling area indicators and contributing to the collection of various insights relevant to the business. At the end of 2018, I graduated VBA and started developing scripts to automate my manual tasks and gain productivity within Excel. In early 2019, I started Power BI and SQL Server and started migrating Excel reports to BI (Business Intelligence) solutions. In 2020 I became a BI Analyst focusing on building strategic, but also tactical and operational dashboards. In 2022 I started in the world of Data Engineering and all the technologies in this framework, together with cloud environments, have been my main focus of learning and evolution at the moment.
